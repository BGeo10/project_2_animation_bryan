Darth Vader's Lightsaber

The background of this weapon is "In a galaxy far far away...". It is called a lightsaber and only the ones strong with "the Force" are able to wield one. This particular lightsaber belongs to Darth Vader. He was once the jedi named Anakin Skywalker and now he has turned to the "Dark Side of the Force" and become a sith lord. He is also one of the most feared sith lords in the galaxy, if not the most feared because of his immense power as "the Chosen One".
